Farming

Farming is the act or process of working the ground, planting seeds, and growing edible plants.
You can also describe raising animals for milk or meat as farming.
Farming is a great way to describe the lifestyle and work of people whose jobs are in the agriculture industry.

Farming according to crops :

  • Arable: Crops
  • Pastoral: Animals
  • Mixed: Crops and animals
  • Subsistence: Grown just for the farmer and his family
  • Commercial: Grown to sell
  • Intensive: High inputs of labour or capital ususally small
  • Extensive: Low inputs of labour or capital
  • Sedentary: Permanently in in one place
  • Nomadic: The farmers move around to find new areas to farm
